Contribution guidelines
  1. Use approved prompts. Generate images via Medical Prompt Studio → ChatGPT UI. Free-hand prompts often miss RadLex/ACR terminology.
  2. Match modality contrast. CT: dense bone bright, air dark. MRI T2: CSF bright, fat dark on fat-sat. US: probe-side up, cone-shaped field.
  3. Label with RadLex terms. Use the autocomplete from our dictionary; only add new structures if you can cite an anatomical reference.
  4. One anatomical view per image. No multi-panel figures. Each image should be a single axial/coronal/sagittal slice.
  5. Educational only. No patient data, no identifying features. All uploads land in the review queue — nothing publishes automatically.
⚠ Educational use only — not for diagnosis or clinical decision-making. All uploaded content becomes part of the OmniRad teaching atlas under the platform's usage terms.
